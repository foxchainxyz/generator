What are your Token requirements?

Comprehensive

Ensure your Token complies seamlessly with the LEV20 standard, making it compatible with any LEV20 wallet globally. Recognition is based on its name, symbol, and decimal value.

Burnable

Exercise the option to burn your tokens, reducing the circulating supply by choosing to destroy a portion of them.

Mintable

Empower yourself to create tokens through minting, a privilege exclusive to the token owner. Minting can be turned off if you wish to cease token production.

Pausable

Exercise control over Token transfers by temporarily halting them. Useful for freezing transfers or delaying trades until a specified time.

Ownable Access

Grant ownership to your token, providing the owner with the authority to conclude minting or generate new tokens.

Role Based Access

Define roles for your token, allowing addresses to be assigned or revoked ADMIN or MINTER roles. Your token also becomes possessable.

Capped

Set a maximum token cap, preventing the generation of more tokens than specified. This instills confidence that you will not exceed your promised token limit.

Payable Token

Enable the LEV20 compatible token to make a callback on the receiver contract, notifying of token transfers or approvals.

Token Recovery

Recover any LEV20 token accidentally sent into the contract, preventing permanent loss to smart contracts. This recovery capability is reserved for the contract owner.
